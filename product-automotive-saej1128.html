SAE J1128 Standard Requirements

Siechem GXL/SXL cables are manufactured in an AS9100D, ISO 9001 and ISO 14001 certified facility. All wires are 100% spark-tested and meet RoHS EU Directive.

GXL — General Purpose Cross-linked

Thin-wall XLPO insulation for general vehicle wiring applications. Smaller outer diameter and lower weight than standard PVC alternatives. Available in bare and tinned conductor variants.

SXL — Special Purpose Cross-linked

Thicker-wall XLPO for demanding underhood and special purpose applications. Provides greater mechanical protection for environments with higher abrasion or vibration exposure.

EBXL Technology

Electron beam crosslinking creates a three-dimensional polymer network that prevents melting at elevated temperatures, improves cut-through resistance, and maintains flexibility in cold environments down to −40°C.

EBXL Production & Testing

Electron Beam Crosslinking

High-energy electron bombardment permanently crosslinks the XLPO insulation polymer chains, eliminating melt-flow at rated temperatures and significantly improving cut-through resistance.
