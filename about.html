What Makes Us Different

Core Capabilities

In-house materials science, proprietary compounding and end-to-end manufacturing. Held by very few wire producers worldwide.

01

Electron-Beam Crosslinking

Our E-beam irradiation facility delivers superior thermal endurance, mechanical strength and chemical resistance. A process held by only a handful of global wire manufacturers.

02

LSZH & Halogen-Free Compounds

Proprietary low-smoke zero-halogen formulations for tunnel, rail and public-space applications. Full compliance with EN 50525 and BS 7655 across the product range.

03

High-Temperature Insulation

PTFE, silicone, ETFE and Kapton-insulated wires rated from −65 °C to +260 °C. Aerospace, industrial furnace and EV powertrain applications.

04

Custom R&D & Rapid Prototyping

Dedicated materials team develops application-specific compounds. From spec sheet to validated prototype in weeks, not months.

05

Full Testing Infrastructure

NABL-accredited in-house lab. Every reel is tested for resistance, hi-pot, insulation IR, hot-set and dimensional tolerance before dispatch.

06

Global Certifications

TUV, UL, CSA, CE, RoHS, REACH, IEC, MIL-Spec, BS EN, IS / BIS. The compliance documentation your project specs require, on file.

Journey

22 Years of Building Wire

2002

Founded in Chennai

Siechem Technologies Pvt. Ltd. established with a focus on specialty wires — a gap in the Indian electrical components market dominated by imports.

2005

Pondicherry Manufacturing Facility

50,000 sq.ft state-of-the-art production line goes online — automated extrusion, stranding, twisting and screening operations.

2008

First MIL-Spec Approval

Defence Materials Quality Assurance (DGAQA) certifies Siechem cables for Indian armed forces applications.

2011

Electron-Beam Crosslinking

India's first wire-grade E-beam facility commissioned. Enables PW 125, Sietherm 155 and high-temperature product lines.

2014

TUV Solar Certification

TUV 2PfG 1169 and EN 50618 certifications secured. Siechem solar cables enter European markets.

2017

Facility Expansion to 100,000 sq.ft

Doubling of production capacity. Addition of fluoropolymer (PTFE/FEP/PFA) extrusion lines.

2020

EV & Battery Cable Range

Launch of EV charging, battery management and high-voltage powertrain wiring qualified to ISO 6722, LV216 and JASO D611.

2024

22 Million Part Numbers

Catalogue surpasses 22 million part-number configurations. Exports cross 50 countries.
