What Makes Us Different

Core Capabilities

In-house materials science, proprietary compounding and end-to-end manufacturing. Held by very few wire producers worldwide.

01

Electron-Beam Crosslinking

In-house E-beam irradiation facility — one of very few in India — delivers superior thermal endurance, mechanical strength and chemical resistance for high-spec cable products.

02

In-House Compound Manufacturing

Proprietary compounding facility for PVC, XLPE, LSZH, silicone and specialty polymer formulations — enabling full control over material properties and mix consistency.

03

Govt. Recognised R&D

Siechem's in-house R&D is recognised by the Govt. of India, Ministry of Science & Technology. ~5% of annual revenue reinvested in R&D projects every year.

04

Cable Design Studio

2D and 3D cable design capability with a full team of engineers, MBAs and PhDs. Customer-specific designs developed for weight reduction, enhanced flexibility and application-specific performance.

05

Electro Tinning

In-house electro-tinning operations for tinned copper conductors across all wire sizes — ensuring solderability, corrosion resistance and contact reliability for connector-terminated applications.

06

End-to-End Manufacturing

Wire drawing, bunching, stranding, insulation, laying up, sheathing, taping, braiding, armouring, jacketing and printing — all under one roof in Pondicherry with machinery from Europe, Russia and USA.

Journey

Over Two Decades of Building Wire

2002

Founded in Chennai

Siechem Technologies Pvt. Ltd. established with a focus on specialty wires and cables for defence, aerospace and industrial applications — a segment largely dominated by imports at the time.

2005

Pondicherry Manufacturing Facility

State-of-the-art automated production facility commissioned at 104/8 & 105/7, Sedarapet Main Road, Pondicherry — spanning the complete production chain from wire drawing to jacketing.

2008

Defence & MIL-Spec Approvals

Cables certified for Indian armed forces applications. Siechem begins supplying to defence, aerospace and railway sectors under DGAQA and RDSO approvals.

2011

Electron-Beam Crosslinking Facility

In-house E-beam irradiation plant commissioned — enabling proprietary high-temperature product lines including PW 125 and Sietherm 155. One of very few such facilities in India.

2014

In-House Compound Manufacturing

Proprietary compound formulation facility operational — providing full control over PVC, XLPE, LSZH and specialty polymer compounds across the product range.

2017

Facility Expansion to 100,000 sq.ft

Pondicherry plant doubles to 100,000 sq.ft of automated production floor. Addition of fluoropolymer (PTFE/FEP/PFA/ETFE) extrusion lines and electro-tinning capacity.

2020

R&D Recognition by Govt. of India

Siechem's in-house R&D department recognised by the Ministry of Science & Technology, Govt. of India. Team expands with PhDs, engineers and technocrats focused on next-generation compounds.

2024

23 Million Part Numbers Across 34 Segments

Product catalogue surpasses 23 million part-number configurations across 34 market segments. Production reaches ~10,000 km of wires and cables per day. Zero-debt financial position maintained.

2026

New 300,000 sq.ft Facility — Bhiwadi

Under construction — 300,000 sq.ft facility in Bhiwadi, Rajasthan (65 km from Delhi Airport), with projected capacity of 5 million metres per day (~1.5 million conductor km/year). Operations scheduled to commence December 2026.
