Layered for the Long Haul

Three engineered layers: Class 5 stranded tinned copper conductor, electron-beam crosslinked polyolefin insulation, and UV-stabilised XLPE outer sheath. Each layer optimised for its job.

  1. 01

    Tinned Copper Conductor

    Class 5 flexible stranded, tinned to IEC 60228. Optimised for low DC resistance and corrosion resistance.

  2. 02

    E-Beam XLPO Insulation

    Electron-beam crosslinked polyolefin. Thermal, chemical, mechanical resistance well beyond standard PVC.

  3. 03

    XLPE Outer Sheath

    UV-stabilised, halogen-free, fire-retardant. Cyclical thermal endurance to +120 °C continuous, +200 °C short-circuit.

PRIMARY

TUV 2PfG 1169 / 08.2007

German solar cable specification. Mandatory for European installations. Includes 1500h UV ageing, hot-set, mechanical strength, and 3rd-party witness testing.

PRIMARY

EN 50618:2014

European Standard for low-voltage cables for photovoltaic systems. Harmonised across EU member states.

IEC 62930:2017

International equivalent. Required for IECEE CB scheme markets including India, Middle East, SE Asia.

UL 4703

US/Canada standard for photovoltaic wire and cable. Required for NEC code-compliant installations.

RoHS 2 / RoHS 3

EU Directive 2011/65/EU + 2015/863/EU. Restriction of hazardous substances in electrical equipment.

REACH

EU Regulation 1907/2006. SVHC declaration on file. No restricted substances above 0.1% w/w.
