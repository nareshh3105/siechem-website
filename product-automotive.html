Manufacturing Excellence

  • EBXL (Electron Beam Cross-Linking) technology for superior thermal performance
  • Unilay conductor stranding for uniform electrical characteristics
  • Lead-free, RoHS-compliant insulation materials
  • 100% electrical continuity testing on every reel
  • Voltage withstand tested per applicable standard

Certifications & Approvals

  • TÜV Rheinland tested and certified
  • Research Design & Standards Organisation (RDSO) approved
  • ISO 9001 Quality Management System
  • IATF 16949 Automotive Quality Standard
  • AIS / CMVR type approvals for Indian market

Conductor Materials

  • E-Cu ETP1 (IEC 60228 / JIS C 3102 / ASTM B3) bare copper
  • Tinned copper option for corrosion resistance
  • Class 5 fine-wire stranding (extra flexible)
  • Class 6 ultra-fine stranding for highest flexibility

Standard Reel Lengths

  • 500m reels: small cross-sections (≤1.25mm² / fine wire types)
  • 1000m reels: medium cross-sections
  • 2000m reels: ultra-thin wall types
  • Custom reel lengths on request
  • Drum packing for large cross-sections (≥25mm²)
