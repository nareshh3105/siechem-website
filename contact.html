Before You Ask

Common Enquiries

What's your minimum order quantity?

For standard catalogue products, MOQ is 100 m (small sizes) to 500 m (large sizes). For custom developments, MOQ is typically 1,000 m after prototype validation.

How long for a custom cable prototype?

From signed spec to validated prototype: typically 4–8 weeks. Includes compound trials, sample production, and full electrical + mechanical test reports.

Can you ship to my country?

We currently export to 50+ countries. Pricing in USD or EUR, terms FOB Chennai / CIF destination. RoHS, REACH, CE certifications included.

Do you provide samples?

Yes — qualification samples up to 2 m of standard catalogue products are free. Custom samples are quoted per request. Most samples ship within 5 business days.

How do I become a distributor?

Send a distributorship enquiry above. We evaluate based on territory coverage, application focus, existing customer base and technical capability. Most regions are open.

What payment terms do you accept?

Standard terms: 30% advance, 70% before dispatch. For long-term customers, NET 30/60 with credit insurance. Wire transfer (RTGS, SWIFT), L/C accepted.
