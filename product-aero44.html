Compliance & Military Standard Testing

Siechem Aero 44 cables are manufactured in a facility conforming to ISO 9001, AS9100D, and ISO 14001, and are 3rd party approved to defense standards.

MIL-W-81044 & AS22759

Specifies dual-wall polyalkene/PVDF insulation. Tests include cold bend (-65°C), high temperature aging (300°C wrap), shrinkage, wick test, and voltage withstand.

DEF STAN 61-12 Part 18

UK Defence Ministry standard for equipment wires. Confirms high reliability under vibration, pressure, and exposure to aviation fuels.

Scrape Abrasion and Cut-Through

Provides 40% greater cut-through and scrape resistance than traditional thick-wall wires, permitting airframe weight reduction without risking electrical failures.
